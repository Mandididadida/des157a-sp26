Thoughts of the Article

The form should be short and simple, which will seems more easier for user to fill, also put the easy part like name or age thing in front that hint "you could just tap in things with out thinking", and put the complex chart at the last several steps like enter what you like and the reason, something like that will makes the reader think "i already filled a lot, this might be the last step" or they already get familiar with filling forms, so they will be more willing to fill out. Also giving selection is better for taping things in form, although i might not save time, but it saves brain power that users could just click, their hand don't needs to get off from their mice.
